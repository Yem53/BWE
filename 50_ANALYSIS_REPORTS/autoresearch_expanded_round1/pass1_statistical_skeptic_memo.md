# Statistical Skeptic Round 1 Memo

## Statistical Position
The revised policy is valid: sample size greater than 15 is enough for inclusion in analysis, but not enough for high confidence. The right treatment is tiered confidence, bootstrap/permutation review, and neighborhood stability.

## Checks To Preserve
- Bootstrap confidence intervals.
- Permutation-null comparison.
- Multiple-testing penalty.
- Effective sample size report.
- Neighboring parameter stability before claims of reusable edge.

## Sample Policy
The updated sample policy is statistically reasonable for discovery: sample_size greater than 15 can enter analysis, but confidence must be tiered. A sample of 16-29 can motivate a probe; it cannot carry the same conclusion weight as 100+.

## Falsification Tests
- Permute timestamps for the same complete strategy object.
- Remove the top 1 percent and top 5 percent contributors.
- Re-score by cluster representative rather than every near-duplicate.
- Compare p10 and stressed median, not only mean or median.
